Email Generator - Oak Creek Trail

While working at Oak Creek Trail, I built an email generator that automates the creation of certain kinds of client emails. The email generator pulls information from the client's API, populates a handlebars template, then produces a ready-to-send email.

Technologies used include Node.js, Handlebars, and AWS Lambda/S3/API Gateway.
